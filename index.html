Reiner Pope on Dwarkesh Podcast: AI Infrastructure Deep Dive

A self-study companion to Reiner Pope's blackboard lecture on Dwarkesh Patel's podcast.

Reiner Pope (CEO of MatX, formerly TPU architecture at Google) sat down with Dwarkesh Patel for a two-hour blackboard lecture covering everything from why batch size dictates inference cost, to how GPU racks are physically wired, to whether models today are 100× over-trained relative to Chinchilla-optimal.

This site walks through fifteen of the load-bearing concepts from that conversation. Each page is a standalone tutorial that starts from zero knowledge and builds to a level where you could read the original papers comfortably. Read them in order or jump to whatever's interesting.
